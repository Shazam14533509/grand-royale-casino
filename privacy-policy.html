Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Grand Royale Casino ("the App") is a free-to-play simulated casino game for entertainment purposes only. No real money is involved. No real gambling takes place.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All game data is stored locally on your device using localStorage.

No Server Communication

The App operates entirely offline. It does not connect to the internet, does not use analytics, does not use advertising, and does not communicate with any external servers.

Data Stored Locally

The following data is stored on your device only:

  • Player name (chosen by you)
  • Password (stored as a one-way hash, never in plain text)
  • Virtual chip balance
  • Game statistics (games played, wins, losses)
  • Achievement and quest progress
  • Game preferences and favorites

Children's Privacy

The App is rated 17+ due to simulated gambling content. It is not intended for children under 17.

Data Deletion

Delete all data anytime by clearing app data in Android Settings or uninstalling the App.

No Real Money

This App uses virtual currency only. No real money can be wagered, won, or lost. Virtual chips have no real-world value. There are no in-app purchases.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, analytics, or advertising.
